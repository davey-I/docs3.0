General

Long: Being long on a stock, means making money as the chart is going up.
Short: Meake money as the stock is going down the chart. ( Figure out how ?? )
Bull: Think the market goes up, expect a Long situation
Bear: Think the market goes down, expect a Short situation

Bid, Ask and Spread: Refere to the prices which Buyers and Sellers are willing to trade their stock.
Bid ist the highest a buyer is willing to pay for its stock. Ask is the lowest that a seller is willing to sell its stock.
The delta between those two is called the Spread.
