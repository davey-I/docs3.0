-  
Bridgemode
: Bridgemode means that e.g. a router/modem, which also has an internal firewall,
   offers the possibility to "deactivate" it, so to speak, so that all traffic is simply authorized.


 - Why should you do this? -> If, for example, you want to use your own firewall
   behind it, as was the case with Tocco during the conversion...., Fritzbox unfortunately did
   not have such a bridge mode.
